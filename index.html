How cool can visually impaired skiers and snowboarders be?

Visually impaired snowboarders and skiers can be incredibly impressive because their path requires courage, strength, and a huge amount of personal effort. On the slope, they don’t just ride fast — they often rely on a guide’s voice and trust their own experience and reaction. These athletes train just as seriously as sighted competitors: they develop technique and the ability to make instant decisions. Their performances prove that limitations don’t prevent people from achieving great results. They become an example that by believing in yourself and moving forward, it is possible to overcome any challenge.
